Spicy Baked Shrimp


Shrimp marinated in a spicy sauce and baked to perfection.


Prep Time:
10 mins
Cook Time:
10 mins
Total Time:
20 mins
Servings:
4

Ingredients

1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
1/4 cup olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tablespoon paprika
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1 lemon, sliced

Directions

Step:1
Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
Step:2
In a large bowl, combine shrimp, olive oil, garlic, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper. Toss to coat.
Step:3
Transfer shrimp to a baking sheet. Top with lemon slices.
Step:4
Bake until shrimp are pink and cooked through, about 10 minutes.
Nutrition Facts (per serving)
200 Calories
10g Fat
1g Carbs
35g Protein
