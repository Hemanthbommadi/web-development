Chicken Shawarma Salad


A delicious and healthy salad made with chicken shawarma, lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and a tangy yogurt dressing.


Prep Time:
20 mins
Cook Time:
10 mins
Total Time:
30 mins
Servings:
4

Ingredients

For the chicken shawarma:
2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup lemon juice
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tablespoon ground cumin
1 tablespoon paprika
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
1/2 teaspoon ground cloves

For the salad:
1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
2 tomatoes, chopped
1 cucumber, chopped
1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
1/4 cup chopped fresh mint

For the dressing:
1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup lemon juice
1 clove garlic, minced
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Directions

Step:1
In a large bowl, combine chicken, olive oil, lemon juice, red wine vinegar, garlic, cumin, paprika, salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper, cinnamon, allspice, and cloves. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight.
Step:2
Preheat grill for medium-high heat.
Step:3
Grill chicken until cooked through, about 10 minutes per side. Let rest for 5 minutes, then slice into thin strips.
Step:4
In a large bowl, combine lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, parsley, and mint.
Step:5
In a small bowl, whisk together yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, cumin, salt, and black pepper.
Step:6
Divide salad among 4 plates. Top with chicken. Drizzle with dressing.
Nutrition Facts (per serving)
450 Calories
18g Fat
34g Carbs
40g Protein
