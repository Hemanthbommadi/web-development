Sweet and Easy Corn on the-Cob


Corn on the cob cooked in a sweet and savory sauce.


Prep Time:
5 mins
Cook Time:
10 mins
Total Time:
15 mins
Servings:
4

Ingredients

4 ears corn, husked and silked
1/4 cup unsalted butter
1/4 cup honey
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Directions

Step:1
Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
Step:2
In a small saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Stir in honey, mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper.
Step:3
Grill corn, turning occasionally, until tender and lightly charred, about 10 minutes.
Step:4
Brush corn with sauce. Serve immediately.
Nutrition Facts (per serving)
250 Calories
15g Fat
30g Carbs
10g Protein
