Chicken Makhani (Indian Butter Chicken)


Chicken cooked in a creamy tomato sauce with butter and spices.


Prep Time:
20 mins
Cook Time:
30 mins
Total Time:
50 mins
Servings:
4

Ingredients

1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tablespoon ginger, grated
1 tablespoon garam masala
1 teaspoon turmeric
1 teaspoon cumin
1 teaspoon coriander
1/2 teaspoon chili powder
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 (13.5 ounce) can coconut milk
1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into chunks
Salt and pepper, to taste
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
1 lime, sliced

Directions

Step:1
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion, garlic, and ginger; cook and stir until onion is tender, about 5 minutes.
Step:2
Stir in garam masala, turmeric, cumin, coriander, and chili powder; cook and stir for 1 minute.
Step:3
Stir in diced tomatoes and coconut milk. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, until flavors are blended, about 15 minutes.
Step:4
Add chicken to the skillet. Cook until chicken is opaque and flakes easily with a fork, about 10 minutes.
Step:5
Stir in butter until melted. Season with salt and pepper. Stir in cilantro and lime slices.
Nutrition Facts (per serving)
450 Calories
25g Fat
30g Carbs
35g Protein
