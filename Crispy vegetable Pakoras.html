Crispy Vegetable Pakoras


Vegetables coated in a chickpea flour batter and fried until crispy.


Prep Time:
15 mins
Cook Time:
10 mins
Total Time:
25 mins
Servings:
4

Ingredients

1 cup chickpea flour
1/2 cup water
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1/2 teaspoon coriander
1/2 teaspoon turmeric
1/2 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1 cup mixed vegetables (such as cauliflower, potatoes, spinach, and onions), chopped
Oil, for frying

Directions

Step:1
In a large bowl, whisk together chickpea flour, water, salt, cumin, coriander, turmeric, chili powder, and baking powder.
Step:2
Add vegetables to the batter and stir to coat.
Step:3
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
Step:4
Working in batches, add battered vegetables to the skillet and fry until golden brown and crispy, about 2-3 minutes per side.
Step:5
Drain on paper towels.
Nutrition Facts (per serving)
300 Calories
15g Fat
40g Carbs
10g Protein
