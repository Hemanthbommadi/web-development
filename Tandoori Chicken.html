Tandoori Chicken


Chicken marinated in a mixture of yogurt, lemon juice, and spices, then grilled to perfection.


Prep Time:
20 mins
Cook Time:
30 mins
Total Time:
50 mins
Servings:
4

Ingredients

1 cup plain yogurt
2 tablespoons lemon juice
2 teaspoons ground cumin
2 teaspoons ground coriander
1 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon ground turmeric
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon black pepper
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 (1 inch) piece fresh ginger root, minced
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
1/2 cup chicken broth
1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Directions

Step:1
In a large bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, cumin, coriander, cayenne pepper, turmeric, salt, and black pepper. Add chicken and toss to coat. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight.
Step:2
Preheat grill for medium-high heat.
Step:3
Thread chicken onto skewers, discarding marinade. Brush with oil.
Step:4
Grill chicken, turning occasionally, until cooked through, about 10 minutes.
Step:5
In a large skillet, heat oil over medium heat. Add onion, garlic, and ginger; cook and stir until onion is tender, about 5 minutes.
Step:6
Stir in tomatoes, tomato sauce, chicken broth, and cilantro. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, until flavors are blended, about 15 minutes.
Step:7
Serve chicken over rice; top with sauce.
Nutrition Facts (per serving)
450 Calories
18g Fat
34g Carbs
40g Protein
